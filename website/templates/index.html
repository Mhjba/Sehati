What are the causes of hearing loss

If he suffers from some infections such as: meningitis, measles, mumps, or an infection that affects the ear and is accompanied by secretions; Use of certain medications; Head trauma or ear injury; Exposure to loud sounds in different places; Listening to personal radios at loud volume; Blockage of the auditory canal by wax or foreign bodies

What are the benefits of donating blood?

On the health level Increases the activity of the bone marrow in producing new amounts of blood. Increases blood circulation activity; It reduces the level of iron in the blood, which reduces the risk of heart disease and clogged arteries. People who donate their blood at least once a year are less likely to develop leukemia. On the moral level The donor’s sense of security and satisfaction thanks to performing his duty and contributing to helping those in need;

How are fats selected?

What are fats? Fats are considered one of the essential nutrients in regulating the functions of the human body. It is a group of compounds that contain fatty acids. Fats work in partnership with some special proteins in the synthesis of lipoproteins present in the blood, where different fats are transported from the blood to different organs of the body. We can differentiate between: Simple fats: triglycerides and cholesterol; Complex lipids: phospholipids including lecithin.

High blood pressure

· High blood pressure represents a major public health problem. It is a chronic disease and one of the most common risk factors for the heart and arteries, which is constantly increasing due to the increasing rate of aging among the population. · High blood pressure, a serious condition that greatly increases the risk of heart, brain, kidney and other diseases. It is a major cause of premature death globally. · The danger of high blood pressure is that it may remain high for many years without any symptoms appearing . If this disease is examined too late, this increases the risk and the high incidence of complications when the disease is discovered. ·
